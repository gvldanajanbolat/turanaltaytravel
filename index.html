The Sacred Altai & Tavan Bogd

The Altai Mountains are the "Golden Pillars" of the world. Standing at the junction of four nations, the Altai represents Earth's rawest majesty. Our expeditions focus on the Tavan Bogd (Five Holy Peaks), home to Khuiten Peak (4,374m) and the massive Potanin Glacier. It is a land where the Great Spirit still speaks through the ice and the wind.

Master of the Skies: Golden Eagle

Hunting with Golden Eagles is an ancient art that has survived for over 4,000 years. Live with the 'Burkitshi', learn their calls, and witness the incredible bond between the nomad and the king of birds. A spiritual dialogue that defines the Altai soul.
