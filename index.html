The Legend of the Golden Eagle

"Бүркіт — аспан патшасы, еркіндік пен батылдықтың бейнесі."

The ancient art of Eagle Hunting is a legacy that has survived for over 4,000 years in the heart of the Altai Mountains. This is not a mere tourist attraction; it is a profound spiritual connection between a human and a wild predator. At Turan Altay Travel, we offer you an exclusive opportunity to sit beside the legendary 'Burkitshi' (Eagle Hunters), to hear the whispers of the wind as the eagle takes flight from the mountain peak, and to feel the raw power of nature on your own arm.

This tradition represents the core of nomadic identity—respect, patience, and absolute trust. You will learn the secrets of how these majestic birds are trained, how they become part of a family, and how they protect the nomadic heritage against the test of time.

The Sacred Altai Tavan Bogd

"Алтай — адамзаттың бесігі, рухтың мекені."

Altai Tavan Bogd, the Five Holy Peaks, stand as silent guardians of the Mongolian frontier. This is where the earth meets the heavens. Our expeditions take you to the Potanin Glacier, the longest in the country, and through valleys where the air is so pure it heals the soul. Standing at the peak, looking over the borders of Mongolia, Russia, and China, you realize the true meaning of eternity.

Turan Altay Travel ensures that your journey through these rugged terrains is safe, professional, and deeply immersive. We don't just show you the mountains; we tell you the stories of the spirits that reside within them, the legends of the ancient warriors, and the wisdom of the nomads who have called this majestic wilderness home for millennia.

Great Nauryz Festival 2026

Experience the rebirth of the world! Nauryz is the celebration of the spring equinox, a time when the nomadic soul rejoices as the winter fades. Join us for the most spectacular festivities: Golden Eagle competitions, the legendary 'Kokpar' (Horse Polo), archery, and the warm hospitality of Kazakh families sharing the sacred 'Nauryz Koje'.
