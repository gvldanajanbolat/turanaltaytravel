Ancient Whispers: The Altai Heritage

Dating back 12,000 years, these prehistoric rock carvings illustrate the early hunting and domestic life of ancient tribes.

Mystical Deer Stones

Magnificent Bronze Age megaliths featuring flying deer carvings, representing the spiritual transition between earth and sky.

Discover the secrets of the Iron Age Scythian nomads, world-famous for their intricate tattoos and royal carpets.
